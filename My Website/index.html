Solomon Outdoors

Hunting

Hunting has always been a passion of mine, I enjoy being outside in God's creation, chasing after the wonderful wildlife my home state Montana, has to offer. I love hunting because, it provides me with lots of food, I am able to get away from the business of life and schedules, I get to connect with wildlife and nature, and I love the amount of skill and effort it takes to harvest and animal in it's natural habitat

Fishing

Fishing has also been a big passion of my life. I do all sorts of fishing, fishing from the shore, fishing on a kayak, fishing in the water, fishing with a fly rod, fishing with a bobber and much more. My favorite fishing is when I hike into an alpine lake deep into the woods and fish small mountain lakes that hardly ever get fished. I love the feeling of being away from everything and everyone and just being outside peacfully enjoying what God has given us to enjoy.
