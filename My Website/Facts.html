White-tailed deer

  • Deer can smell humans from over half a mile away.
  • Deer can run up to 40 miles per hour!
  • Their Antlers fall off every year and regrow for the rut.
  • They are essentially red and green colorblind.
  • They can see about 300 degrees because of their wide set eyes!
